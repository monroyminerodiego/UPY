Amazon and ICT: How Technology Enables the Business Model

Who is Amazon and what is its business model?

Amazon evolved from an online bookstore to a global digital ecosystem. Its model integrates four pillars: marketplace (connecting sellers and buyers), Prime subscription (loyalty and logistics), AWS (cloud infrastructure), and advertising (data monetization). This diversification allows it to capture value at multiple points in the digital chain, from transaction to the infrastructure that supports it.

How do ICT enable this business model?

ICT is the invisible engine: cloud computing (AWS) provides global scalability; AI/ML personalizes experiences and optimizes logistics; big data generates behavioral insights; IoT and robotics automate warehouses; edge computing and CDN accelerate delivery. Each technology amplifies the model's capabilities, transforming data into sustainable competitive advantages.

What indicators show Amazon's digital scale?

The numbers reveal the scope: 300+ million global Prime users, AWS with 32% cloud market share, advertising revenue growing 20% annually, and presence in 200+ countries. These indicators demonstrate how Amazon has built a digital ecosystem that transcends geographic and sectoral boundaries.
