üî¨ The Science Behind It

üéØ Why does this happen?

Popular people appear in more friend lists! When you look at your friends, you're more likely to be connected to people who have many friends (popular people) than to people who have few friends (less social people).

Think of it like this: if someone has 100 friends, they appear in 100 different people's friend lists. If someone has only 2 friends, they appear in just 2 friend lists. You're 50 times more likely to be friends with the popular person!
